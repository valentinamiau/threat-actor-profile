# LockBit Ransomware Group

## Overview
LockBit is a ransomware-as-a-service (RaaS) group known for targeting organizations across multiple industries using double extortion tactics.

## Motivation
- Financial gain
- Data theft and extortion

## Common Targets
- Small and medium-sized enterprises
- Healthcare
- Manufacturing
- Professional services

## Initial Access
- Phishing emails
- Exploitation of exposed RDP services
- Compromised credentials

## Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures (MITRE ATT&CK)
- Initial Access: Phishing (T1566)
- Execution: Command and Scripting Interpreter (T1059)
- Persistence: Scheduled Task/Job (T1053)
- Impact: Data Encrypted for Impact (T1486)

## Analyst Notes
LockBit operations often rely on initial access brokers and prioritize rapid execution.
